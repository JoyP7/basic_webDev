Medal

A Medal is a character-based object, holding skills and Special Attacks one can utilize in Kingdom Hearts Unchained χ / Union χ. They are the physical manifestation of power from the future, drawn by the Book of Prophecies.

Attack Medals are equipped to one's Keyblade deck, allowing one to perform more powerful attacks than one's Keyblade alone can inflict. Depending on the strength of one's Keyblade, players can equip a maximum of 5 Medals, a 6th medal on their Pet slot, and make use of an Assist Medal.

Support Medals are used to improve your Attack Medals, either by leveling up, evolving, or adding skills.

Buff Medals are used to greatly increase your damage output; Debuff Medals are used to reduce your enemies' defense. The buffs and debuffs can be stacked up to 7 times each.
  • Strength can be raised/lowered by up to 7 tiers.
  • Defense can be raised/lowered by up to 7 tiers.

There are separate strength and defense parameters for individual attributes as well. So you can also raise/lower attribute-based strength/defense by up to 7 tiers in addition to the regular strength/defense buffs and debuffs listed above. There are also strength and defense parameters for Upright and Reverse medals as well.

Special Attacks

A Special Attack ("Ability") is an attack attached to each Medal that requires a certain number of gauges to use. When the player has enough gauges, these Special Attacks can be unleashed by swiping the Medal toward the center of the screen. Special Attacks can be single target, all target, or random target, referring to the number of enemies struck by the attack. Some rare Medals might have an all-target Special Attack which is normally single target; these Special Attacks will have a higher gauge cost to activate.

Special Attack Bonus

The Special Attack Bonus ("Guilt") is a trait found on 6★ Medals which have been strengthened with the Power Bangle, which is obtained in Quest 34. A 6★ Medal's Special Attack Bonus can be unlocked by strengthening it one more time after the 5 dots have been filled in. The Special Attack Bonus is a damage bonus added on top of the damage multipliers the player earns by strengthening their Medal.

Different Medals have different Special Attack Bonus Tiers, which determine the range of the bonus received. When a Special Attack Bonus is applied, a value in that range is randomly chosen. By strengthening the medal with Fantasia Mickey B or an additional copy of that 6★ Medal, the Special Attack Bonus will be recalculated. If the new value is higher than the previous value, it will be kept, otherwise the previous value will be retained. Common Medals will have Level 1 Bonus, Normal Medals will have Level 2, Rare Medals will have Level 3, and Super Rare Medals will have either Level 3, 4, 5, 6, 7 or 8.

Nova

Nova ("Burst") is a type of attack that deals massive damage with no attributes to all targets but can only be used once per quest or Raid Boss battle. Nova can be used once the player has unlocked the Special Attack Bonus of at least one Medal. It can be activated by pressing the button on the top right of the screen at any point during the player's turn before the last Medal is played. The higher the Nova level, the more damage it deals.

Skills

A Skill is a special passive ability that rarely appears on Medals drawn from the Shop deals, or which are added to Medals by fusing with a Scrooge Medal. Scrooge Medals can be found in Coliseum Boards, Premium Boards, or rarely as quest objectives (usually for Event Quests).

Skills can be transferred from one Medal to another through fusing. The Medal in the first slot has a chance of passing its skill on to the base Medal, starting at 20% at skill level 1 and increasing as the skill levels up to 100% at max level; Scrooge Medals will always have their skill level at maximum, barring a bug. Pressing the "Overwrite Skill" button in the fusing process turns this feature off (it is on by default).

To level up a skill, simply use the Medal as you fight enemies. Skills have a chance of activating when a Medal is used, or when a special condition is met. As a skill levels up, it has a higher chance of being triggered.

For skills that lower the cost of a Medal's special attack, the player must have enough gauges remaining for the special attack's original cost to activate it.

Trait

A Trait is a special passive ability that can be obtained by fusing two of the same Medal together. The trait assigned to the Medal is randomly decided.

Every Medal has one or more slots to hold traits. When fusing another Medal to an existing Medal that has its trait slots filled beforehand, you can choose to overwrite one of the old traits for the new one, or keep them as they are.

All traits are stackable, with the exception of "Extra Attack: 40% Power", "Ground Enemy DEF -60%" and "Aerial Enemy DEF -60%".

Trait Medals

Trait Medals are a special type of Medals that can add, or re-roll the trait, of one specific Medal.

Trait Medals appear as grayed-out versions of the Medal that they affect in the form of 6★ Medals, and share that Medal's Album number. They have an ATK and DEF stat of 1, and a cost of 999. Their Special Attack is called Lesser Attack . It targets a single enemy, costs 1 gauge to perform, and has a multiplier of x1.00.

Each Trait Medal gives 15 Pet Points, and shares the score bonus from events like the High Score Challenge with its affected Medal. Despite being meant to use like Boost Medals, they can be combined with EXP and Boost Medals like a normal Medal, and can even unlock their Special Attack Bonus.
